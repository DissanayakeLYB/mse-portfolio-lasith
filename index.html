About Me

Hello! I'm Lasith Dissanayake, a passionate Materials Science and Engineering undergraduate with a focus on nanomaterials and photovoltaic materials development. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree at University of Moratuwa, expected to graduate in June 2025.


My academic journey has been driven by a fascination with how materials behave at atomic scales and how we can engineer them to solve real-world problems. I'm particularly interested in biomaterials and their applications in medical devices.


When I'm not in the lab or studying phase diagrams, you can find me playing chess, watching a Sitcom, or volunteering with STEM outreach programs for school students.
